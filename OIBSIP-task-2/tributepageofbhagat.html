A brief history of Bhagat Singh's journey

early life

Bhagat Singh was born in the year 1907 in a small village called Banga in punjab province of colonised india. he was a charismatic Indian revolutionary[6] who participated in the mistaken murder of a junior British police officer in what was to be retaliation for the death of an Indian nationalist.

revolutionary activities

in the year 1928 british government set up simon commission which was made to gice the ideas for the changes in the rules.he was against it with other freedom fighters like lala lajpat rai, chandrashekhar azad.their protest led to the lathi charge on them and assaulted lalaji during charge by the police.they made a plan to kill the police man who was involved in lalaji's attempt to kill but mistakenly killed john saunders.

HSRA

HSRA stands for hindustan socialist republican association which was catered by the youth of india and they always wanted complete independence in every terms of the whole indian subcontinent.they always emphasized on the requirement of economic equality which would have given the actual freedon and not just to achieve a level of independence.

delhi assembly bombing and execution

in the year 1929 bhagat singh with batukeshwar dutt went to bomb in the assembly proceedings in delhi just to grab the attentions of the global media during the trials so that they can set up their ideology of getting over the colonization by the help of youth. in the jail they also started the hunger strike which was made a clear statement against the oppresion of britishers on indian political prisoners Singh, Rajguru and Sukhdev were sentenced to death in the Lahore conspiracy case and ordered to be hanged on 24 March 1931.The schedule was moved forward by 11 hours and the three were hanged on 23 March 1931 at 7:30 pm in the Lahore jail.
